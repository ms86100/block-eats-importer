## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Greenfield Community is a private marketplace exclusively for Shriram Greenfield residents. Connect with your neighbors for everything from homemade food to professional services.

🏠 COMMUNITY ONLY
This app is restricted to verified residents of Shriram Greenfield. Each user is verified by community administrators before gaining access.

🍱 FOOD & GROCERIES
Order homemade meals, bakery items, snacks, and fresh groceries from your neighbors. Support local home chefs and vendors in your community.

📚 CLASSES & LEARNING
Find tutors, yoga instructors, dance teachers, music lessons, art classes, and coaching - all from verified community members.

🛠️ HOME SERVICES
Book trusted electricians, plumbers, carpenters, AC technicians, and appliance repair services from within your community.

🏠 DOMESTIC HELP
Find reliable maids, cooks, drivers, and nannies recommended by your neighbors.

💇 PERSONAL SERVICES
Access tailoring, laundry, beauty services, mehendi artists, and more - all from trusted community providers.

🚲 RENTALS
Rent equipment, party supplies, baby gear, and more from neighbors. Save money and reduce waste.

📦 BUY & SELL
List pre-owned furniture, electronics, books, toys, and household items. Buy directly from neighbors you can trust.

🎉 EVENT SERVICES
Book catering, decoration, photography, and DJ services for your celebrations - all from community professionals.

🐕 PET SERVICES
Find pet food, grooming, pet sitting, and dog walking services from pet-loving neighbors.

🛒 KEY FEATURES
• Browse sellers by category (Food, Services, Rentals, Classes, etc.)
• View seller ratings and reviews from real neighbors
• Real-time order tracking and notifications
• In-app chat with sellers
• UPI and Cash on Delivery payment options
• Favorites and order history
• Book time slots for services
• Rent items with date range selection

👩‍🍳 BECOME A SELLER
Residents can apply to become sellers and share their skills, products, and services with the community. All sellers are verified and approved by administrators.

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
Your data stays within the community. We only collect information necessary for order fulfillment and community verification. See our full Privacy Policy in the app.

📱 SUPPORT
Need help? Access the Help & Guide section in your profile for tutorials and FAQs.

---
This app requires community verification. Non-residents will not be able to access the marketplace features.
```
